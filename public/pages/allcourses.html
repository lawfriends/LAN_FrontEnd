All courses

Our language school is proudly offering free courses to anybody who wants to improve thier italian skills. There are courses of 3 levels, each of them having 2 classes each week. The courses are supported by volunteer teachers, that are dedicating their time to help anybody get more familiar with the italian language, culture and civilization.

The lessons are modulated according to the needs of each class. Students are not required to purchase materials, excercises and teaching materials are provided by the School. Students are required to attend at least 80% of the lessons.

Select a course if you want to find out more about them.

Introductory course

Learn how to get around and understand daily conversations.

Basic course

Learn how to understand italian used in different context.

Intermediate course

Speak italian and converse easily with locals.
