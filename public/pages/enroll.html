How to enroll

The enrolment in language courses opens 2 times a year, once in September, and another time in January. If you want to join one of the courses, you have to follow these steps:

1. Check when to enroll

You can find the enrolment periods upper on this page.

2. Fill in the form or call

You can directly fill in the form with your contact data, and we will get back to you with an appointment for attending the language test.

3. Confirm appointment

You will receive an email with a confirmed appointment, and you should reply to it with your acceptance or request to change the appointment date and hour. We try to be as flexible as possible, but we still have to respect a limited time frame.

4. Take the language test

Join us at our office 10 minutes before your appointment. Our colleagues will welcome you in and will explain what is the test procedure.

5. Receive confirmation

After assessing your Italian language level, you will be sent a confirmation email that will inform you about the beginning of the lessons, date and time when they are held. You should confirm through a reply to this email that you wish to be part of the course, thus finalizing your enrollment.
