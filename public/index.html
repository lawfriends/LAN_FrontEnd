The italian language school "InSieme" takes shape thanks to the help of about 20 volunteers, from different social and cultural backgrounds, and follows these different guidelines: Empathy, Patience, Flexibility and Heart.

Our courses

Courses are our association's core service. At InSieme, volunteers guide students through three levels of proficiency, from the very first steps to an intermediate level, to help them in integrating with life in Milan.
